How to simulate a Farm

For this project we will be simulating a beef farm's buisness. We will be using javascript to create the program. Html and CSS can be utilised in order to display the results of the simulation and provide an interface as desired once you have the simulation working.
The beef farm industry is vastly complex with hundreds if not thousands of outside variables constantly affecting the decision making process and the outcomes of events. We will be aiming to simulate the main events that have the majority of impact on the farm's buisness. The key variable we will be looking into is the money made or lost by the farm. Therefore the aim is to track the events that change the amount of money the farm may produce.
